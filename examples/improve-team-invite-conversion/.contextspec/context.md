# Product Context

## What we build

Tessera is a team workspace tool for product and engineering teams of 5–50 people. It combines a shared issue tracker, lightweight docs, and a cross-functional planning view in one workspace.

## Stage

Post product-market-fit, early growth. ~1,200 paying workspaces. Annual revenue is small but growing month over month. The team is 8 people.

## Target user

A product or engineering lead at a 5–50 person product team who is the buyer, the inviter, and usually the workspace admin. Their teammates are the invitees we care about for this initiative.

## What we are optimizing right now (2026 H1)

1. Invite conversion: more invited teammates becoming activated members within 7 days.
2. Time-to-first-collaborative-action: how long after signup an invitee creates or comments on something a teammate sees.
3. Free-to-paid conversion of workspaces with at least 5 activated members.

Items 1 and 2 are upstream of item 3, so most growth work this half is concentrated on the invite and activation surface.

## Where this fits

Tessera sells to small teams, so a workspace is essentially worthless until at least three people are active in it. Anything that helps the inviter pull more of their teammates over the activation line directly drives revenue. This is why invite conversion is treated as a product surface, not just a growth tactic.
